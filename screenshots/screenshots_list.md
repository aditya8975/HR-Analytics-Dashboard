## Recommended screenshot specs
- Resolution: 1920x1080 or your native screen resolution
- Format: PNG (not JPG - keeps text/chart lines crisp)
- Crop tightly to the dashboard canvas, avoid OS chrome/taskbar
- Keep consistent slicer selections across screenshots for a cohesive look
